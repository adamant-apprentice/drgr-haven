What Is APO Time?

APO time is the official game time. All events, resets, and timers follow APO time ‚Äî not your local phone time.

  • APO time is shown at the top of your game screen.
  • Daily missions reset at 00:00 APO.
  • Event start and end times always use APO time.

You can also toggle between APO time and your local time. This helps you see exactly when an event starts in your own timezone, so you don‚Äôt have to calculate it yourself.

How to See Time in Last Z:

If you miss events often: you are probably looking at your local time instead of APO time.

Why APO Time Matters

Understanding APO time helps you:

  • Know when daily missions reset.
  • Plan for Alliance Duel days and other events.
  • Show up on time for big fights like Capital and State Ruler.

Once you get used to checking APO time, you will stop missing rewards and start planning your growth better.

Three Events Many Players Mix Up

These three events sound similar but are very different: Capital Clash, State Ruler, and Alliance Duel.

Understanding the difference will help you know when to fight, when to follow server rules, and when to focus on points.

Capital Clash ‚Äì Practice Capital War

Capital Clash is a warm-up event that happens before State Ruler.

  • Everyone on the server can fight.
  • Fights happen around the Capital.
  • Rewards are decent but not the biggest.
  • Used to practice Capital fighting and coordination.
Think of it as: ‚ÄúPractice round for the real Capital war.‚Äù

State Ruler ‚Äì The Real Capital War

State Ruler is the main Capital event. Your whole server fights another server.

  • Goal: capture or defend the Capital.
  • Biggest rewards and strongest buffs.
  • Requires coordination and following server rules.
  • Happens at the end of the event cycle.
Think of it as: ‚ÄúThe real war for control of the server.‚Äù

Alliance Duel ‚Äì Alliance vs Alliance Points

Alliance Duel is a weekly event where your alliance competes against another alliance.

  • You score points by doing daily tasks (Hero Day, Research Day, etc.).
  • Only your alliance and the enemy alliance are involved.
  • No Capital fighting ‚Äî it‚Äôs about points, not territory.
  • Runs for a full week with 24-hour windows to contribute.
Think of it as: ‚ÄúWeekly competition to see which alliance is more active and efficient.‚Äù
